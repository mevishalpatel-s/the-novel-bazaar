ABOUT OUR BOOKSTORE

THE NOVEL BAZAAR

Welcome to The Novel Bazar - a place where stories live forever and every book holds a heartbeat. We believe that books are not just pages bound together, but emotions, memories, and journeys waiting to be discovered. From the oldest timeless classics that carry the wisdom of generations to the latest novels that capture today’s dreams, our shelves are filled with stories that connect hearts across time.
At The Novel Bazar, you will find novels in Hindi, English and Urdu because we understand that every reader feels at home in a different language. Whether you are searching for a childhood favorite, a rare vintage treasure, or a new story to lose yourself in, we are here to help you find that special connection.
We keep our prices affordable because we believe everyone deserves the joy of reading. A good book can change a day, a mindset, even a life and we want that magic to be within everyone's reach.

The Novel Bazar is not just a bookstore — it's family for readers.

Best Seller Of The Year

That Long Silence

by "Shashi Deshpande"

Description: Jay's life comes apart at the seams when her husband is asked to leave his job while allegations of business malpractice against him are investigated. Her familiar existence disrupted her husband's reputation in question and their future as a family in jeopardy, Jaya, a failed writer, is haunted by memories of the past. Differences with her husband, frustrations in their seventeen-year-old marriage, disappointment in her two teenage children, the claustrophobia of her childhood and mdash; all begin to surface. In her small suburban Bombay flat, Jaya grapples with these and other truths about herself and mdash; among them her failure at writing and her fear of anger. Shashi Deshpande gives us an exceptionally accomplished portrayal of a woman trying to erase a 'long silence' begun in childhood and rooted in her and in the constraints of her life.
